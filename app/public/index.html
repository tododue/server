WHAT IS to-do/due?

Your all-in-one planner solution. In the age of online learning, there are many sites to be visited to make a schedule. This website and extension attempts to solve this problem by aggregating that data.

AUTOMATIC

Able to automatically find your assignments, to-do/due allows you to see everything in one place (as it should be).

CUSTOMIZABLE

Written with the user in mind, to-do/due allows for customization of your workspace. Rename, change, and have a good time doing your assignments!

INTUITIVE

Written by three teenagers and fueled by caffeine, this site is fast, responsive, and...yeah
